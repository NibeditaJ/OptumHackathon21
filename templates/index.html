What is AUTISM or ASD

Autism, or autism spectrum disorder (ASD), refers to a broad range of conditions characterized by challenges with social skills, repetitive behaviors, speech and nonverbal communication. According to the Centers for Disease Control, autism affects an estimated 1 in 54 children in the United States today.

Facts

1. Around 5.4 million adults in the US are autistic.

2. One in 54 American children is autistic.

3. Around 40% of autistic children do not speak.

4. ASD is four times more likely to occur in boys than girls.

5. Older parents are more likely to have autistic children, autism statistics remark.

6. Mothers diagnosed with diabetes have a 48% chance of having autistic children.

7. Autism statistics reveal that the COVID-19 pandemic increases anxiety in autistic children.

8. families with an autistic child underwent an average loss of 14% in their entire family income
